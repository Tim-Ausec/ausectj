Agrovolo

17.09.2025 23:28

Agrovolo is a one-stop-shop solution for aerial imaging data targeted at agriculture / land management use cases.

Background

Precision agriculture is the practice of utilizing data to make informed land management decisions. This approach to land management promises to make farming operations more efficient and environmentally friendly by identifying overuse of resources, such as fertilizers and irrigation. However, current solutions are often piecemeal. In the particular case of aerial imaging data, the hardware that collects the data and the software that processes the data are not fully integrated.

Mission

To develop an autonomous UAV that utilizes remote sensing techniques to create a high-quality map of the Normalized Difference Vegatative Index for any plot of land at any time.
